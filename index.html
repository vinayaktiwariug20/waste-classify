üöÄ The "Colab to Production" Journey

Most machine learning models begin and end in a notebook. My goal was to ensure this model was accessible and testable.

  • Model Selection: Evaluated four CNN backbones (ResNet50, ResNet100, EfficientNetB0, and VGG16); EfficientNet strictly outperformed candidates across all metrics.
  • Optimization: Implemented L2 regularization, batch normalization, and a 20% dropout rate to ensure robust feature extraction
  • Real-World Testing: The live demo allows users to bypass pre-cleaned datasets and test against "in-the-wild" images.
